# Annual Leave Policy - FTE_CN_GZ

Document ID: AL-FTE-CN-GZ-2026
Applies To: Full-time employees (FTE) in Guangzhou, China
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

## 1. Leave Unit
Annual leave can be requested in increments of 0.5 day.
Requests below 0.5 day are not accepted in the HR leave system.

## 2. Advance Notice
For planned annual leave of 1 to 3 days, employees should submit at least 2 calendar days in advance.
For planned annual leave of more than 3 days, employees should submit at least 7 calendar days in advance.
If there is an urgent personal reason, manager may approve an exception with written note.

## 3. Maximum Consecutive Days
Employees can take up to 10 consecutive annual leave days in one request.
Any request longer than 10 consecutive days requires HRBP review and Director-level approval.

## 4. Approval Rules
- 0.5 to 2 days: direct manager approval is required.
- More than 2 days up to 5 days: direct manager plus skip-level manager approval is required.
- More than 5 days up to 10 days: direct manager, skip-level manager, and HRBP approval are required.
- Over 10 days: handled as an exception case, requiring HRBP and Director approval.

## 5. Blackout and Operational Constraints
During company-declared blackout windows, annual leave may be restricted for business-critical teams.
Managers can defer leave dates when minimum staffing requirements are not met.

## 6. Carry-over Note
Unused annual leave carry-over is governed by local labor law and separate payroll policy.
This document only defines request and approval workflow.
